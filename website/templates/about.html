Our Story

Our journey began in the summer of 2010, when two childhood friends decided to turn their passion for ice cream into a dream business. With nothing but a small freezer and a handful of family recipes, we started serving our first scoops in a tiny storefront on the corner of Maple Street.

Word quickly spread about our creamy, homemade ice cream, made from the freshest local ingredients. We experimented with flavors, mixing traditional favorites with unique creations, and soon our little shop became a beloved spot for families, friends, and ice cream enthusiasts from all around town.
